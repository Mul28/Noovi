# Noovi Growth Email Types

## welcome

Use for a fresh lead that has enquired and needs the first Noovi response.

Expected next step:
- wait for intake form completion or reply

## intake_reminder

Use for a lead that has already received the welcome email but has not yet completed intake.

Expected next step:
- wait for intake form completion or reply

## preview_ready

Use when a preview exists and the issue includes `Preview URL`.

Expected next step:
- wait for approval or revision requests
